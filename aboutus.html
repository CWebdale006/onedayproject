About Us

This company was originally created in 2020 as a school project for our West-MEC program but since then, it has grown into so much more. We created the website in a day for a programming challenge, and have continued to improve on it since.

Why pencils?

When we were brainstorming for products to market, we wanted something simple. One of the first ideas we had was simply: pencils. They have a big market and could be easily advertised, making them the obvious choice.
